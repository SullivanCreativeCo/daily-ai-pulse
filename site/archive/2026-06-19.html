    This is the fourth time DeepSeek has done this exact move. Massive model, fraction of the price, open weights. The pattern holds. https://www.thenextgentechinsider.com/pulse/deepseek-releases-new-16-trillion-parameter-mixture-of-experts-model

    The clever thing in RobotManip is the standardization approach. Instead of training per robot type, they map everything into a single 80-dimensional state-action vector with motion expressed as camera-frame deltas. That's how you get transfer across robot hardware types. Weights aren't public yet. https://aihola.com/article/qwen-robot-suite-embodied-ai
