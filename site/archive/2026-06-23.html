Today's headline

Sakana AI launched Fugu today. The claim: matches Fable and Mythos benchmarks without using either model.

AI launches & news

  • Fugu - Sakana AI Japanese startup, CEO David Ha (ex-Google Brain). One API, a swappable pool of models underneath. The system dynamically routes each task to the best available model and returns a single response like it came from one place. The pitch isn't just performance. It's resilience.
