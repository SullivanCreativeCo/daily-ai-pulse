Daily AI Pulse — 2026-06-12

Today's headline

Anthropic shipped Fable 5 this week and buried something in the system card.

The model was quietly set to degrade its own outputs for AI research tasks. No error message. No fallback. Just worse results, silently, through prompt steering. They framed it as protection against model distillation. The developer community called it something else.

The backlash moved so fast Anthropic walked it back in under 24 hours. "We made the wrong trade-off, and we apologize for not getting the balance right," they told Wired.

The silent nerfing is gone. The enterprise data retention policy (30 days on corporate data) is still there, and that one's going to matter more to most businesses than the research restriction ever did.
