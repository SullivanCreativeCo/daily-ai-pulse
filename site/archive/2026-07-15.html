LI . NO. 02
PrismML shipped a 27B model that runs on a phone.

3.9GB footprint. Multimodal. Does reasoning and coding. Runs locally.

The thing everyone's missing: this isn't just a feature. It's a cost inversion.

Cloud APIs charged you per token. Per request. Per million tokens. The math pushed you to keep models small and stateless and push computation upstream.

On-device flips that. Your processing is free after you download the model. No API calls. No latency. No request scaling.

For most small businesses, that changes the unit economics of AI entirely.

You don't care about inference speed anymore. You care about local privacy and the fact that every query costs you zero after the initial download.

Same with latency. Your model doesn't need to talk to a server. It talks to your device.

This is why Mistral's robot model works with a single RGB camera. Local inference. No API. No cloud round trip. Just predict, act, repeat.

The game changed. And most people haven't realized the scale of it yet.
