If your company runs Claude in Slack, August 3 is a real date.

Anthropid is retiring the old integration. The replacement is Claude Tag. Admins who don't migrate before August 3 get auto-migrated whether they're ready or not.

The migration is worth understanding before it happens to you - it's not a chatbot upgrade. It's a different product.

Old integration: one AI per user. You ping it like a chatbot.

Claude Tag: one AI instance per channel. It builds shared memory across everyone in that channel over time. With the right admin setting, it monitors conversations without being tagged and surfaces things it judges relevant to the team.

One ambient agent with organizational memory sitting in your most active channels is a fundamentally different thing than a chatbot each person uses separately.
