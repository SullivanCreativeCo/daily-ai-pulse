"People are really starting to use voice to interact with AI, especially when they have a lot of context to dump."

That's not a feature observation. That's a bandwidth observation.

Typing caps you at maybe 40 words per minute if you're fast.

Talking gets you to 150.

The constraint on agent usefulness isn't the model. It's how fast you can feed it context.

Voice isn't about convenience. It's about getting more signal into the system before the system acts.

So the question isn't whether your agents can talk.

The question is: are you still typing your context in like it's 2023?
