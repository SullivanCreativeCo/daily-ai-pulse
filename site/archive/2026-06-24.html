LI . NO. 02
There's a 5x cost difference hiding in how you're prompting AI right now.

Greg Isenberg and Amir broke it down this week.

Same task. Same output quality. 44 cents with GLM 5.2. $2.38 with Opus 4.8.

The move is model chaining. You don't run everything through the expensive model.

Opus plans the work. Opus reads the screenshot because GLM 5.2 doesn't have vision yet. Then GLM 5.2 executes. A reviewer polishes.

Each model does what it's actually good at.

At small scale, 5x doesn't feel like much. At a hundred tasks a day, it adds up fast. At the team level, it becomes a real line item.

Setup takes about 20 minutes. ZAI API key into Cursor, override the endpoint, add GLM 5.2 as a custom model. OpenRouter works too if you're on Codex.

The Isenberg episode is 22 minutes and worth every one.

If you're running AI daily and paying full freight on every task, at least do the math.
